The Silent Problem in the Crate

A sow's water consumption is a vital sign. Deviations are often the first and only indicator of a problem. But can you spot the danger in time?

CRITICAL LOW

< 0.5 L/hr

A major red flag for illness, pain, or a blocked water nipple. Requires immediate action.

HEALTHY RANGE

0.8-1.5 L/hr

Consistent consumption indicates a healthy, comfortable, and productive sow.

WARNING HIGH

> 3.0 L/hr

A potential sign of fever, mastitis, or infection. Requires investigation.
